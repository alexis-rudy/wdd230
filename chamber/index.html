Beginners Coding Camp

Ever wanted to be a programmer but don't know where to start? Well now you can! With our 8 week program, you'll learn all the basics it takes to start on your journey. Participants will finish the program with a self-made working website, knowledge of HTML and CSS, and a certificate of completion. Sign up here.

Free Lunch at the Park

Starting June 1, free lunch at the parks will start. Any child under the age of 18 can get a lunch that includes milk, a main course, and a side of veggies for free. There will also be activities from the Traveling Playground on certain days!

Lion's Park Pool Opens

Need some relief from the summer heat? Come enjoy a dip at Lions park swimming pool. It's fun for the whole family and tickets start at just $5.
