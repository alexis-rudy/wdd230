Personas

Persona 1 - Recently Relocated

Boise and it's surrounding areas are growing rapidly. Nampa is one of those cities that seems to always have new residents relocating. New members of the city will need a site that they can easily access for information on business, events, and weather. Most new residents of Nampa are parents of families. The site will need to accommodate someone who is trying to quickly find family friendly activities to attend, local sports teams to get their children into, as well as events for just the parents to escape to. This persona will access the site through either a desktop computer, or a mobile device.

Persona 2 - Business Owner

As a business owner in Nampa, keeping up with other businesses is a priority. An old owner would need to know about new businesses opening in the city, any renovations happening, promotions other businesses are running, and which places are shutting down. Additionally, the business owner would need to know of events in the city and whether or not they allow promotion. If the owner is opening a new business, they would need the same information above, as well as information on local business to work with. This persona will access the site through either a desktop computer or a mobile device.

Scenarios

Scenario 1 - 'Who will be my competition if I chose to open this kind of business?

Scenario 2 - 'My little boy wants to play soccer, and my daughter is doing dance. Where would I find places for them to do that?'

Scenario 3 - 'Where's a good place to eat tonight?'

Scenario 4 - 'What's the weather going to be during the parade today?

Scenario 5 - 'Will the farmer's market be open today?'
